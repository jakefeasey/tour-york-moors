The Route

The route starts (and finishes) at the Lion Inn and descends to the south edge of the Moors to start the West section. This first loop takes you west to the Sutton Bank trail, winds up to the northern edge and finally loops back to the Lion Inn.
The second section takes you over the valley on the old railway route, heads south into Dalby Forest for a nip around some single-track, then out to the coast at Scarborough. From here you head north up the Cinder Track to Whitby via Robin Hoods bay. The final chunk of this section is back westwards to Fryup and where the Yorkshire Bike Hub marks the end of the second section.
The final section goes into the most northerly section of the moors. The route goes up to Scaling Reservoir, then west to Guisborough woods and the impressive Roseberry topping. Finally you'll go south over a few more dips and peaks to take you back to the Lion Inn for a some well deserved pub grub and a pint (or several).

Stats:

Elevation Gain/Loss: 5690m
