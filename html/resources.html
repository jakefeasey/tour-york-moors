Resources

There are so many resources available to help plan a trip and also while your out on the trail that listing them all would be impossible.
Instead, I plan on including things that I find most useful or places that I like to stop at along the way.

Maps and Guides

  • The North Yorkshire Moors are covered by OS Maps OL26 (Western area) and OL27 (Eastern Area)
  • Goldeneye Guide for cycling in the North Yorkshire Moors
  • The North Yorkshire Moors website is handy for more general visitor info
  • Hopefully in time we will be putting together a physical map with the route and handy info overlaid.

Youth Hostels

  • Whitby - Top of a big hill (great views, but gotta go up)
  • Boggle Hole - South of Robin Hoods Bay
  • Scarborough - In the city center
  • Cote Ghyll Mill, Osmotherley - About 3 miles off route, but a good town for pubs etc.
  • Dalby - A mile or two off the trail, not much in the way of resupply options

Dalby Forest Trail Center

  • Paid for Car Park - no overnight parking
  • Bike shop
  • Bike Wash Station
  • Outside Drinking water tap (can get frozen/turned off in winter)
  • Cafe - The one behind the bike shop is better but check opening hours in advance.
  • Toilets

High Paradise Farm

  • Cafe - One of the best and most friendly on route!
  • Self-catered Rooms
  • Camping Site

Yorkshire Cycling Hub

  • Cafe
  • Bike Shop
  • Bike Hire
  • Shower and Changing facilities
  • Parking
  • Camping Pods
  • Accomodation

Sutton Bank Visitors Center

  • Paid for Parking
  • Bike Shop
  • Bike Wash Station
  • Cafe
  • Toilets
  • Large (Bus) Shelter (I think)

Lord Stones Country Park

  • Cafe
  • Restaurant
  • Farm Shop
  • Glamping Pods

Guisborough Forest Visitors Center

  • Cafe
  • Paid for Parking
  • Picknick Area
  • General Shop
